About

From hardware
to intelligent systems.

I'm Sri Krishna Kalyan — an ML Engineer whose path started in mechanical engineering, moved through mechatronics and embedded systems, and arrived at full-stack AI and computer vision. That cross-disciplinary background isn't incidental — it means I understand the hardware my models run on, the systems they integrate with, and the engineering constraints that matter in production.

At the University of Adelaide's Auto-ID Lab, I worked on autonomous UAV navigation — combining YOLO-based detection with ROS2 planning and VINS-Fusion odometry. Before that, I built and deployed a production defect segmentation pipeline at Proto Corp. Earlier still, I deployed a neural network entirely on a microcontroller — 97% accuracy, no cloud, 11 FPS.
